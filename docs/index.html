The Legend of Zelda Text Analysis

The Dark World and The Hero's Descendants Timeline

This is a project that will analyze a specific branch of the Zelda Timeline. There are six games in this timeline, but we will be analyzing the games below and comparing them. The reasons for this are at the bottom of the page.

  1. The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time
  2. The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask
  3. The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess
  4. The Legend of Zelda: Four Swords Adventure

Please Note: There are several games that are a part of the timeline that will not qualify for this assignment. These games are Link's Crossbow Training, Hyrule Warriors, and Breath of the Wild. There are no files containing the text for Crossbow Training and the latter two are recent releases, so we are refraining from those games to avoid potential copyright problems.

Similarly, Skyward Sword and The Minish Cap will be excluded from this assignment because they predate this branch of the timeline. Ocarina of Time predates this branch as well, but it is the breaking off point for the timeline, so it cannot be disqualified.
